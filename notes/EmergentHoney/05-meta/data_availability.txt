Data Availability Statement

The repository accompanying this manuscript includes:

1. a canonical result bundle used for all manuscript tables and figures;
2. scripts that regenerate the published tables and figures from that bundle;
3. a prototype simulator implementing the pheromone-driven self-organization logic.

This version of the paper reports an SDN-informed simulation/emulation study.
It does not claim that all reported results come from a completed production
deployment or a full hardware-backed deployment campaign. No external datasets were used.

Upon acceptance, the artifact will be released as an open-source repository.
